Briads

A HEART WRAPPED IN FUR

the Briads or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep

Breed History

The Braid, which is believed to be originated in france, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French armies as sentries, messengers and to sense wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing. Briads were used in the first world war almost to the point of extinction. Currently the population of briads is slowly recovering
