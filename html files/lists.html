Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is the recipe fo deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbs Butter
  • 2tbs Cream

Method

  1. Melt butter over frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using spatula, fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you're making an omelete
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (It will still cook on the plate until served)
